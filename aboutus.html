About Us

Our History

We believe free healthcare should be provided everywhere. However, as we know, getting medical assistance can be expensive for many people around the world. Many people avoid reaching for medical services due to their heavy expenses. Therefore, we created a platform to offer 24/7 online healthcare to anyone for free. Our mission is to help as many people as we can and save lives.

Hire Volunteers

Do you want to help people in countries that have no access to medical services and possibly save lives? Hackcare Wellness is the place for you. Working at Hackcare Wellness will provide you with valuable medical experience by treating patients around the world.
